As NFTs have evolved they have bben used to sell art. This website charts the rise of the NFT art gallery.

2022 - predictions

  • EIP2981 is widely adopted
  • ERC725
  • NFTs representing AIs common place
  • NFTs with a 3d file format to support integrations into the metaverse common place
  • A triple A game title uses NFTs
  • People don't care what chain NFTs are minted on
  • Everything that can be tokenised has been tried
  • Metaverse terminology goes mainstream due to people learning about NFTs
  • Physical and digital NFTs are common place
  • It's commonplace for NFTs to be shown in galleries on screens or projectors
  • POS is the norm for blockchains supporting NFTs
  • NFT artwork assets as well as metadata increasingly on chain (e.g. on Arweave)
  • There is exhibition about the history of NFTs
  • Everyone's gran knows about NFTs
